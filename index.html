Enterprise Features

Botpress Enterprise is tailored for the needs of large organizations who need to handle a large volume of requests while complying with high standard of security & privacy.

Workspaces for collaboration

Workspaces can be seen as a logical unit that groups up your conversational assistants. You may create as many bots as you need within a workspace.

Multilingual Assistants

(12 built-in languages)
You are no longer restricted to supporting only one language per chatbot.

Role-Based Access Control

you may create roles and assign them to collaborators to grant them the permissions and privileges they need to accomplish their work.

Security

Activating your Enterprise license will allow your business systems to integrate with more advanced and secure authentication methods such as:SAML, LDAP, OAuth

Clustering

you can deploy as many nodes (Botpress instances) as is required to answer increasing demands on the system as your user base grows.

Monitoring

In this dashboard you can find key performance metrics of each Botpress node in your cluster such as CPU and Memory usage, as well as some Botpress specific metrics like bot health.

Alerting

This feature allows you to define rule sets that trigger incident events. These events can be forwarded to your ops team to to quickly take action if required.

Bot Pipelines

This allows teams to work on bots in multiple stages of development in the same way you would any other software product.

Branding

Depending on your needs, you may rebrand the entire Botpress interface.
